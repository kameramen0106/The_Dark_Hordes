The Dark Hordes
===============

**The Dark Hordes** (**TDH**) is an add-on campaign for the
**[Battle for Wesnoth][1]**.

[1]: <https://www.wesnoth.org>

Lead fugitive dark sorcerer Gwiti Ha’atel to mastery of the undead hordes.

**(Intermediate level, 12 scenarios of 17 completed.)**

**NOTE:** This campaign is currently undergoing rework and maintenance.
I am looking for ideas and feedback. Please read the forum thread for more
details on the project.


Installing The Dark Hordes
--------------------------

Released versions of TDH are available from the official Battle for Wesnoth
add-on server; players who know how to use Git may be able to clone TDH
from the [main repository][2] on GitHub.

[2]: <https://github.com/kameramen0106/The_Dark_Hordes>

TDH is still under active development. Keep in mind that significant
changes may be made from version to version and full compatibility may not
be maintained.


Reporting issues
----------------

If you encounter any issues, you may report them at the campaign’s
[development topic][3] on the Wesnoth.org forums or its [issue tracker][4]
on GitHub.

[3]: <https://r.wesnoth.org/t58445>
[4]: <https://github.com/kameramen0106/The_Dark_Hordes/issues>

Be aware that depending on the nature of the problem, you may be asked to
provide a saved game file to reproduce it.
